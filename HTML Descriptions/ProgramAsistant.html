TITLE: Facilities Specialist

GENERAL DESCRIPTION

Ensure that the physical environment of all our centers support the delivery of high quality services to all children and families.

MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES:

  1. Coordinates and maintains all records for the USDA food program at the center.
  2. Tracks all required child health and developmental screenings and maintains accurate records on site and electronically.
  3. Tracks inventory and coordinates purchases for the center, including food service supplies, office supplies, program supplies, diapers & wipes, and health supplies.
  4. Maintains center petty cash fund and completes monthly reconciliation reports.
  5. Keeps office area neat and organized. Maintains clean, inviting space for visitors
  6. Greets staff, parents, and visitors upon arriving and departure from the center. Gives prompt and courteous attention.
  7. Answers the phone. Gives accurate and pertinent information.
  8. Tracks well child checks and immunizations for children at the center and works with parents to maintain up-to-date records.
  9. Maintains accurate and organized child and staff files.
  10. Assists Center Manager and/or Family Support Specialist with data entry of application and enrollment paperwork, child health records, staff records and classroom and child assessments.
  11. In collaboration with Center Manager and Family Support Specialists prepares and distributes monthly center newsletter.
  12. Assists Family Support Specialist with completing enrollment applications for children and pregnant women and maintaining center waiting list.
  13. Participates in the daily maintenance of the site. Follows procedures for reporting maintenance needs in a timely manner.
  14. Assists Center Manager with classroom support and modeling of appropriate interactions as needed.
  15. Assists Family Support Specialist with tracking attendance.
  16. Assists Center Manager and Family Support Specialist with tracking non-federal share.
  17. Performs other job relation functions as required.
